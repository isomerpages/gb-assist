### Application Details

<p>Licence for Import/Transhipment of Fresh Fruits and Vegetables is required if Traders involved in the import/transshipment of fresh fruits and vegetables.<br /><br />Licence Pre-requisites :<br />1.Top Up CPF Medisave.<br />2.Activate UEN with Singapore Customs.<br />3.Submit original GIRO form.</p>
