### Application Details

<p>Licence is not required for the listed public entertainments or arts entertainment activities provided that the organiser adheres to the exemption conditions. Otherwise, organiser is to apply for the appropriate public entertainment or arts entertainment licence.</p>

### Documents Needed

Town Council Agreement Letter
Letter from Town Council

Constituency Letter
Constituency Letter if the event is ‘Constituency Event'



### Requirements

Limited Liability Partnership
Applicant must be having position:
* PARTNER cum MANAGER

Company
Applicant must be having one of the following position:
* MANAGING DIRECTOR
* SECRETARY
* DIRECTOR
* CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Business (Sole Proprietor/
Partnership)
Applicant must be having one of the following position:
* OWNER
* AUTHORIZED REPRESENTATIVE

LP
Applicant must be having one of the following position:
* GENERAL PARTNER
* LIMITED PARTNER
